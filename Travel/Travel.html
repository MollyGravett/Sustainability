Could you go #flightfree2020 ?

One of the most effective things we can do to reduce our carbon emissions is to fly less. If aviation was a country, it would be the 7th largest contributer to pollution in the world. According to the Guardian UK, Brits fly more than any other nation, and a return flight from London to Edinburgh contributes more CO2 than the mean annual emissions of a person in Uganda. In the midst of a global climate crisis, it's time to make a change. And this one could not be easier.
